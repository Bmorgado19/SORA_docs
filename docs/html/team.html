About Us¶

The SORA package was developed with support of the ERC LuckyStar, that agglomerates the efforts of the Paris, Granada, and Rio teams. The ERC LuckyStar is funded by the ERC (European Research Council) under the European Community’s H2020 (2014-2020/ERC Grant Agreement No. 669416). Also, this project is supported by LIneA (Laboratório Interinstitucional de e-Astronomia), INCT do e-Universo (CNPQ grants 465376/2014-2), by FAPESP (proc. 2018/11239-8) and by CNPQ (proc. 300472/2020-0), Brazil.
